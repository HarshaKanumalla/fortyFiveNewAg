VEHICLE

I DON’T SEE THE SUSPENSION ON THE VEHICLE. WILL THE RIDE BE COMFORTABLE?
Wide and high aspect ratio tires offer improved stability while assuring a smooth ride over bumps. With proper inflation pressure, there is no need for suspension on any kinds of terrain. Also, a sensor is being incorporated into tires which notifies the user whenever the pressure is not optimum.
WON’T THE FAT TIRES HAVE TROUBLE ACCELERATING?
With good inflation pressure, tires provide enough grip for the bike to accelerate. Apart from this, the motor itself generates enough torque to accelerate the bike effortlessly.
WILL I NEED ANY SPECIAL PERMITS, REGISTRATION OR LICENSE TO RIDE THIS BIKE?
No, you won’t need any special permits, registration and license to ride this bike. As per government norms, electric bikes equipped with motor of 250W power and speed limited to 25kmph don’t require any special permits, registration or license.
WHAT ARE THE BIKE SPECIFICATIONS?
The dimensions for the bike are 60’’ x 38’’x15’’. Standard model has a 250W motor and a 500WH battery. The top speed of bike is 25kmph and it reaches top speed in 5 seconds.
I SEE THAT THE BATTERY IS EXPOSED. IS IT SAFE IF IT RAINS OR IN CASE OF A CRASH?
The battery case is designed to withstand crash or harsh weather conditions such as rain, dust, temperature etc. The battery also has a layer of thick foam wrapped around it in order to protect it from shocks and impacts.
DOES THE BATTERY DRAIN WHEN THE BIKE IS NOT RUNNING?
The battery consumes power only during throttle. However, when other devices such as head lights etc. are switched on, minimal power will be drawn.
